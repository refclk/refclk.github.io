Recommendation

For Resistor

Tolerance %E series
Wider than ±20%E3
±20%E6
±10%E12
±5%E24
±2%E48
±1%E96(Some vendors provide E24+E96)
Tighter than ±1%E192(Some vendors provide E24+E192)

For Capactior

The E series like E48 or above are typically not applied, and therefor the tightest tolerance of 1% is used with values of E24 series.
